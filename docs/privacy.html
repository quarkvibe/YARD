YARD Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 23, 2024

YARD ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Profile Information: If you join the Rec Yard community, you may provide a display name, handle, bio, and profile photo.
  • Social Links: Optional links to your social media profiles (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, YouTube, Discord, Threads).
  • Workout Data: Exercise counts, completion times, and workout history that you choose to submit to public leaderboards.

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and analytics.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve our features.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the YARD app and its features
  • To display your profile and workout submissions on public leaderboards (Rec Yard members only)
  • To process your subscription and payments through Apple/Google
  • To communicate with you about updates or issues
  • To improve our app and develop new features

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With other Rec Yard community members (your public profile and workout submissions)
  • With service providers who help us operate the app (Supabase for database, RevenueCat for subscriptions)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Update or delete your profile information at any time
  • Request deletion of your account by contacting us
  • Opt out of the Rec Yard community while still using core app features

Children's Privacy

YARD is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Photos and Camera

YARD requests camera and photo library access solely to allow you to set a profile photo for the Rec Yard community. Photos are stored securely and are only visible to other community members. You can use the app without granting photo access.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: Payment processing

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@yardfitness.app
