The Workout

Simple. Brutal. Effective.

No complicated routines. No rest timers. Just flip, move, and finish the deck.

Flip

Draw a card from the deck. The suit determines your exercise. The value tells you your reps.

Move

Complete the reps. Pushups or squats. No rest until done. Hit the button to flip again.

Finish

Clear all 52 cards. Track your time. Beat your record. Come back tomorrow and do it again.
