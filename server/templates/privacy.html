Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 21, 2025

YARD is designed with privacy in mind. Your workout data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information.

Information We Collect

YARD is a local-first application. All workout data, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. We do not have access to this data.

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses
  • Location data
  • Workout data or fitness information
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes

Rec Yard Subscription

If you subscribe to Rec Yard (our optional competitive mode), the following applies:

  • Profile information you choose to share (username, profile photo) is stored to enable leaderboard features
  • Workout times are submitted to verify leaderboard positions
  • Payment processing is handled securely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store) - we never see your payment details

Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases
  • RevenueCat (optional): For subscription management, if enabled

These services have their own privacy policies that govern the data they collect.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device:

  • Workout history and personal bests
  • App settings and preferences
  • Deck style selections

This data is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly use Rec Yard social features.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the YARD app from your device
  • Using the "Clear Data" option in the app settings (if available)

Children's Privacy

YARD is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:
