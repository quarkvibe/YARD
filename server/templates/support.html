Frequently Asked Questions

The Workout

How does the deck of cards workout work?

Each card represents reps. Number cards (2-10) are face value, Jacks/Queens/Kings are 10 reps, and Aces are 15 reps. Flip a card, complete the reps, then flip the next. Work through all 52 cards as fast as possible.

What exercises are included?

YARD uses two bodyweight movements: pushups and squats. In Superset mode, spades and clubs are pushups while hearts and diamonds are squats. You can also set all cards to pushups or all squats.

What are the intensity levels?

Misdemeanor is standard (396 total reps). Hard Time applies a 1.5x multiplier (594 reps). Lifer doubles everything (792 reps). Choose based on your current fitness level.

Rec Yard Subscription

What is Rec Yard?

Rec Yard is our optional subscription ($2.99/month) that unlocks competitive features: global leaderboards, weekly challenges, verified workout times, social features, and achievement badges. The core workout functions fully without it.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store settings. On iOS: Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions > YARD. On Android: Google Play > Menu > Subscriptions > YARD. You'll retain access until the current billing period ends.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store), not by YARD directly. Visit their respective support pages to submit a refund request for your purchase.

Privacy & Data

Is my data private?

Yes. YARD stores all workout data locally on your device. We don't collect or access your workout history, settings, or personal information. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

How do I delete my data?

Uninstalling YARD removes all locally stored data. For Rec Yard subscribers, contact support@flipmovefinish.now to request deletion of any cloud-stored profile or leaderboard data.
