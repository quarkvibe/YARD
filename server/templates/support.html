Frequently Asked Questions

How does the deck of cards workout work?

Each card in the deck represents a number of reps. Number cards (2-10) are face value, Jacks/Queens/Kings are 10 reps, and Aces are 15 reps. Flip a card, do the reps, flip the next. Complete all 52 cards as fast as you can.

What exercises are included?

YARD focuses on two bodyweight movements: pushups and squats. In Superset mode, spades and clubs are pushups while hearts and diamonds are squats. You can also do all pushups or all squats.

What are the intensity levels?

Misdemeanor is standard (396 total reps). Hard Time is 1.5x multiplier (594 reps). Lifer is 2x multiplier (792 reps). Pick based on your fitness level.

What is Rec Yard?

Rec Yard is our optional subscription ($2.99/month) that adds competitive features: global leaderboards, weekly challenges, verified times, and social features. The core workout is fully functional without it.

How do I cancel my Rec Yard subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store. On iOS, go to Settings, tap your name, tap Subscriptions, and select YARD. On Android, open Google Play, tap Menu, then Subscriptions.

Is my data private?

Yes. YARD stores all workout data locally on your device. We don't collect or have access to your workout history, settings, or personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

The app crashed or isn't working correctly. What do I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, reinstalling usually fixes it. Contact us at the email above if you continue to experience issues.

Can I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). Visit their support pages to request a refund for your purchase.
