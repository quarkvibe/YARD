Frequently Asked Questions

The Workout
Each card represents reps. Number cards (2-10) are face value, Jacks/Queens/Kings are 10 reps, and Aces are 15 reps. Flip a card, complete the reps, then flip the next. Work through all 52 cards as fast as possible.
YARD uses two bodyweight movements: pushups and squats. In Superset mode, spades and clubs are pushups while hearts and diamonds are squats. You can also set all cards to pushups only or all squats only.
Misdemeanor is standard (396 total reps). Hard Time applies a 1.5x multiplier (594 reps). Lifer doubles everything (792 reps). Choose based on your current fitness level.
YARD offers four flip modes: Fresh Fish (one card at a time), Trustee (2 cards at a time), OG (flip again if under 20 reps), and Pod Father (keep flipping while under 30 reps). Each mode changes the workout intensity and pacing.
Rec Yard Subscription
Rec Yard is our optional subscription ($2.99/month) that unlocks competitive features: global leaderboards, weekly challenges, verified workout times, social features, and achievement badges. The core workout functions fully without it.
Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store settings. On iOS: Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions > YARD. On Android: Google Play > Menu > Subscriptions > YARD. You'll retain access until the current billing period ends.
Refunds are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store), not by YARD directly. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com for Apple or Google Play support to submit a refund request.
Privacy & Data
Yes. YARD stores all workout data locally on your device. We don't collect or access your workout history, settings, or personal information. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.
Uninstalling YARD removes all locally stored data. For Rec Yard subscribers, use the contact form below to request deletion of any cloud-stored profile or leaderboard data.

Troubleshooting

App Not Loading

Force close the app and reopen. If issues persist, try reinstalling YARD from the App Store or Play Store.

Timer Issues

Ensure your device time is set correctly. Disable Low Power Mode as it may affect timer accuracy.

Subscription Not Active

Tap "Restore Purchase" in the Rec Yard tab. Ensure you're signed into the same Apple/Google account used for purchase.

Data Not Saving

Check available storage on your device. YARD requires minimal space but needs write access to save workouts.

Manage Your Subscription

iOS

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select YARD to manage or cancel

Android

  1. Open Google Play Store
  2. Tap Menu, then Subscriptions
  3. Select YARD
  4. Choose Cancel or Update
